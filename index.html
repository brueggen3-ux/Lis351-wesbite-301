History

The history in Hayward Wisconsin is bold and rich. Originating as a busy logging town in 1882

Activities

In Hayward the possibilities are endless. Many lakes offer time in nature to relax. The lumberjack world championship offers exciting fun and main street is a must-go.

Location

Hayward is located in Northern Wisconsin. With many lakes surrounding it; Hayward is a hotspot destination in the summer and a dormant ice fishing getaway.
